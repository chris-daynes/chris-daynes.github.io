6.6 Cultural Blog

When programming, what are some of your triggers? What causes a disproportionate emotional response?

I get triggered when frustration levels elevate. This occurs when I cannot make my programe do what I believe it should or when I am being asked to do a task that I feel completely unskilled to do. To be honest though, I don't really feel big triggers when programming maybe just little triggers.

How did you find the Siberian North Railroad exercise? (Stop, Breath, Notice, Reflect, Respond)

I like this exercise a lot. I have used this in part previously. Well actually just the stop, breath, and respond parts. I like the addition of reflection, looking inward to see why a particular trigger avent occured. There almost always is a personal/historic aspect to it. Putting yourself in the other persons shoes alos helps manage a trigger and dissolve that blaming thing at may occur when a particular trigger involves another person.
