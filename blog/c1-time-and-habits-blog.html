3. What worked for you?

I have really been interested to see the resergence of meditation to enhance proformance. I learnt transendental meditation(TM) a long time ago and found it so good. But I am embarrassed to say that like exercise, it's one of the first things to go when life gets busy. So I am going to put it back. But for those of you who read try meditating for 2mins, that simply won't cut it. It's a 20min twice a day thing. After 2mins your mind will still be racing. Your a meditation master if you can have a calm mind that quickly!
